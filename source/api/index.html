Feed Attributes

Attribute Description 
description
 The name of the product 
card_type_category_logo_url
 Card type logo url e.g. for amex, visa, mastercard 
product_type
 The type of product e.g. credit_card 
interest_free_period_category
 Denomination for interest_free_period e.g. months 
interest_free_period_quantity
 Number of interest_free_period e.g. 12 
late_payment_fee_currency
 Number of late payment fee 
late_payment_fee_frequency
 Period of late payment fee e.g. monthly 
late_payment_fee_percentage
 Percentage of balance for late payment 
level_category
 Card Level e.g. Gold, Platinum 
minimum_repayment_currency
 Minimum repayment currency value e.g. 4 for $4 
minimum_repayment_percentage
 Minimum repayment as a percentage of balance 
purpose_category
 Card purpose e.g. Personal or Business 
purchase_standard_rate_percentage
 Purchase Standard Rate 
introductory_rate_percentage
 Introductory Rate 
introductory_period_category
 Introductory Period Type e.g. Months 
introductory_period_quantity
 Introductory Period Quantity e.g. 3 
best_rate_percentage
 The 'best rate' is the lower of the intro rate (with a intro period greater than 10 months) and the purchase standard rate. 
best_rate_type
 Whether the 'best rate' is the Introductory or the Purchase Standard rate 
annual_fee_percentage
 Annual Fee as a percentage of balance 
annual_fee_currency
 Annual Fee as a currency value 
annual_fee_frequency
 The period of the Annual Fee e.g. yearly
